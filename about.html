About C6 Group

Turning Strategy Into Execution

C6 Group exists to help leaders build stronger organizations by providing executive-level leadership, operational expertise, and practical solutions that turn vision into execution and challenges into opportunity.

Our Purpose

Why C6 Exists

Many organizations do not lack good ideas. They lack the time, experience, or internal resources to consistently execute those ideas well. Small businesses, in particular, often cannot justify hiring full-time executive leadership in every area of their organization, yet they still face increasingly complex operational, financial, and organizational challenges.

C6 was created to close that gap — providing executive-level leadership and operational expertise that can be engaged when needed, so business owners can access experienced guidance without expanding their permanent leadership team.

Rather than being confined to a single industry or business model, C6 applies proven leadership principles across multiple sectors. Strategy, execution, accountability, and operational excellence are universal — allowing C6 to evolve alongside changing industries and new ventures while remaining grounded in the same core philosophy.

C6 exists so business owners can focus on leading and growing their businesses, confident that the operational foundation supporting them is built to succeed.

Leadership

Meet the Managing Partners

C6 Group is led by partners who have spent their careers building organizations, managing complex programs, and solving real-world operational challenges — not developing theory from the sidelines.

Tyler Cooperider

Managing Partner

Managing Partner
